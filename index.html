Matteo's Background

Originally from Milan, Italy, Matteo Mariani began his formal music education at the Conservatory “Giuseppe Verdi” of Milan, where he graduated cum laude with a degree in Jazz Guitar.

His academic excellence and artistic promise earned him a scholarship to the renowned Berklee College of Music in Boston, where he further honed his craft in an international and forward- thinking musical environment.

Studying with such luminaries of the instrument as Oz Noy, Nir Felder and Bobby Broom. Over the years he has shared the stage with musicians of the caliber of Kenny Werner and Kevin Harris among many others.
